OBJECTIVE

he goal was to create online platform to bring athletes and brands(sponsors) all over the world together in one place. The biggest challenge was to give professional mark of athletes’ popularity to give brands best candidates for their promotion campaigns. Also it was challenging task to give brands last news and information about athletes.

Solution

Hungry Season is an online platform that unites athletes and sponsors together. It helps to communicate all players in sport industry quickly, easily and safely.

Benefits

Efficient promotion

Any brand can promote his own products connecting with top athletes all over the world

Safe Deals

When the deal starts the brand reserves funds for an athlete. The athlete takes money only after he finishes promotion campaign.

Fast communicationsn

Brands and Athletes communicate through our platform within built-in messenger.

TOP Althetes and TOP Brands

We connect the biggest companies all over the world with a huge network of athletes.
